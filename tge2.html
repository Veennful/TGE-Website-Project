About us

 

Original Name "Lux Clan" (2017) Rebranded to "Lux Prestige Gaming" (2018) Rebranded to "LNC" (Lux Nation Clan) (2019) Rebranded to "TLNC Gaming" (Team Lux Nation Clan Gaming) (2020) Rebranded to "TLNC Gaming Esports" (2022) Rebranded to "TLNC/TGE" (2023) OG Members/Players (TLNC "Username" from 2017-2022) New Generation/Recruits (TGE "Username" from 2023+)

Veennful(Valance) - Who & What is TLNC? "I founded TLNC Gaming in 2017 to bring fellow gamers together to form a relaxed and enjoyable gaming community. We originated from Call of Duty Black Ops 2, dominating the Search And Destroy, Team Deathmatch, and Ground War playlists. Over time, we've grown into a stronger team and aspire to enter the competitive gaming scene professionally. Our goal is to establish our name and attract skilled players who share our passion for gaming.

 

Dispair(Rose) - Why Should You Join? "TLNC is dedicated to motivating, fostering communication, and transforming your gaming career while you're with us. Whether you aspire to become a competitive gamer, a content creator, or simply enhance your teamwork skills, we provide the support and guidance you need to achieve your goals. I joined this team in 2017, and it has had a profound impact on me and the friends I've made over the years. TLNC Gaming is a game-changer.
